Trust first

Built to be helpful, careful, and clear

Mathia earns trust by keeping context scoped, letting you opt in, and making every nudge explainable.

Safety promises

You are in control

  • Invite-only access; you choose who joins your workspace.
  • Per-room consent for sharing context and reminders.
  • Clear reasons for every proactive nudge.

Short-lived memory

  • Conversation context refreshes regularly to avoid stale or over-shared data.
  • Each room keeps its own memory; nothing crosses rooms or users.

Guardrails on money

  • Safe defaults on payment amounts and recipients.
  • Human approval for sensitive actions and transfers.

Transparency

  • Activity summaries so you can see what ran and why.
  • Easy ways to pause nudges or disconnect integrations.

Common questions

  • Can I stop nudges? Yes, you can pause or mute them per room or per user.
  • How long is context kept? Only as long as needed to keep a conversation coherent, then it resets.
  • How do I invite my team safely? You control invites; each teammate gets their own access and room-level permissions.
