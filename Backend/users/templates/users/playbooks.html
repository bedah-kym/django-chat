Playbooks for real teams

Pick a persona to see ready-to-run prompts and flows. Copy them, tweak them, and ship in minutes.

Choose your lane

Freelancer / Founder

Keep cash and follow-ups moving without adding more tools.

  • "After every client call, draft recap + payment link; schedule follow-up in 2 days."
  • "If an invoice is overdue 3 days, send a gentle nudge and surface the contact thread."
  • "Create a weekly digest of open tasks per client room."

Outcome: Faster payouts, fewer missed replies.

Ops / Support Lead

Stay ahead of stalled work and keep stakeholders aligned.

  • "Every morning, list rooms with overdue tasks and propose owners."
  • "When travel changes, notify finance and update the shared itinerary."
  • "Create a Friday summary of resolved vs. pending issues with next steps."

Outcome: Clear ownership, less chasing.

Growth / RevOps

Repurpose what is working and close loops automatically.

  • "Pick last week's best post and republish to LinkedIn at 9am with a fresh hook."
  • "After a Calendly booking, send prep doc, collect materials, and remind 1 hour before."
  • "When a deal closes, send invoice + thank-you note and log revenue goals."

Outcome: More pipeline touches without more tabs.

Travel / Field Teams

Keep itineraries, alerts, and receipts in sync.

  • "When a flight confirmation arrives, add to trip plan and block the calendar."
  • "Day-of-travel checklist to traveler + finance with per diem and hotel info."
  • "Summarize trip expenses and send a reimbursement reminder."

Outcome: Fewer surprises on the road.
