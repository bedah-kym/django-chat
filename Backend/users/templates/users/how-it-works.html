How Mathia works

Explain what you need in plain language. Mathia carries context forward, suggests the next best action, and executes across your tools.

1) Capture

You describe the job in chat: "Send a recap, create an invoice, and follow up Friday."

  • Mathia collects the key details and keeps them scoped to the room.
  • No forms to wire—just words.

2) Remember

Context stays with the project so you never repeat yourself.

  • If you say "add a return flight," Mathia already knows the outbound details.
  • Each room has its own memory; nothing leaks between clients.

3) Act

Mathia runs the workflow and nudges you when something needs a human decision.

  • Posts to socials, sends payment links, schedules meetings, and updates notes.
  • Explains why it nudged you and what it will do next.

Quick wins to try in the trial

  • "Summarize today’s calls and create follow-ups per client."
  • "Repurpose my top-performing post to LinkedIn at 9am tomorrow."
  • "When a payment is marked done, send a thank you and log revenue goals."
  • "Build a trip itinerary from these confirmations and share with finance."
